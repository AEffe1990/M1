Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your tastebuds with this festive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your new favorite holiday starter!

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 1 3/4 cups (350g) lukewanm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 mmedium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brusgin on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a paring knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cramberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dought

    2. In a medium-sized mixingfg bowl, or in astand mixer with paddle attachment, mix together all the dought ingredients until the flour is full absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dought, thought I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
